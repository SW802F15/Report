Behind the scenes, Tempo Player consists of a number of modules, each playing an important role in the overall functionality of the application. 

In this chapter the Music Player module is described in terms of user stories and implementation, as well as observations made in regards to XP during development of the module. In \Cref{chap:reflectionMethodology} reflections are made on these observations.

This chapter also contains a brief explanation of Android terms which are necessary to understand this chapter.
